ABOUT THIS SITE


Personal finance is an extremely important topic that is rarely touched upon in school. There are so many misconceptions floating around, especially regarding credit cards. My goal is to help debunk some of these rumors, and show you how, even with minimal effort, you too can redeem for free flights, hotels, reduce the stress of traveling, and much more!

Finally, it is very important to understand that there is no one-size-fits-all way to do credit cards. It really depends on your needs and spending pattern. This site will teach you the basics of how credit cards work and walk you through some of the benefits that come along with them. Head on over to the next section to get started!



ABOUT ME

Hi there! I'm a software engineer and urban planner in my mid-20s. Like many of you, I initially stayed away from credit cards because of stories on how they can adversely affect your credit score, the high annual fees, and the ridiculous interests banks can charge. However, don't fret! If you spend responsibly, credit cards can be extremely rewarding!

I've only started this hobby a little more than two years ago, but have already amassed more than a million points & miles, all without spending more than I normally would (I make sure each card I have is 'profitable') while being a student. The secret is to put the right type of spend on the right cards, including your rent, tuition, and other big costs! Many think that you need to have high income and high spend in order to do credit cards, but while spending more does accumulate more points faster, regardless of how much you spend, even a one card setup can help you earn enough for a round-trip economy every year with nearly no effort!
