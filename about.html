About Me

Background

Hi there! I am a 26 year old software engineer and urban planner. Like most of you, I initially stayed away from credit cards because of stories on how they can adversely affect your credit score, the high annual fees, and the ridiculous interests banks can charge. However, don't fret! If done right, credit cards can be extremely rewarding!

I've only started this hobby a little more than two years ago, and have amassed more than a million points & miles, all without spending more than I normally would (I make sure each card I have is 'profitable') while being a student. The secret is to put the right type of spend on the right cards, including your rent, tuition, and other big costs! Many think that you need to have high income and high spend in order to do credit cards, but in reality if you are financially responsible, organized, and determined, you can do it too!

There is no one-size-fits-all way to do credit cards. It really depends on your needs and spending pattern. This site will teach you the basics of how credit cards work and help you maximize your rewards!
